The Best Movies According to John

My top 3 movies of all time.

Fear and Loathing in Las Vagas

This is a cult classic that deals with the struggles of drug abuse and satire.

Fired Up

This movies is about 2 teens that go to cheer camp to hook up with untold amounts of girls. Fun fact... The 2 male stars were in thier 40 when filming.

Porky's (trilogy)

This trilogy is the story of high school seniors who are so desperate to hook up with older women, they try to sneak into an adult bar for sex and booze.
